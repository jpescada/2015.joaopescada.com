What I do

In one sentence: I merge ideas with technology.
Or at least that’s what I like to say. It’s abstract enough to cover most of what I do, yet it specifies the two major components: ideas and technology.

With creative agencies I enhance teams with technical and user experience capabilities and help find the best ways to express digital work for their clients. Find out how I can help agencies 

With startups and small businesses I help defining a solution for the problem at hand, refining it to better serve their customers and growing it to a successful product. Find out how I can help startups 

Depending on your location we can collaborate in-house or remotely.
